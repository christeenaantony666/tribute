Arijit Singh

About

Arijit Singh is a famous Indian playback singer and music composer. Although he has performed in many other Indian languages, he primarily sings in Bengali and Hindi, which are his native tongues. Prior to the 2013 releases of Tum Hi Ho and Chahun Main Ya Naa, Arijit Singh had just begun his career when he took part in the modern reality programme Fame Gurukul in 2005. According to Spotify, he was the most streamed Indian musician in 2020 and 2021. On Spotify, he has the highest number of Asian solo fans. Arijit Singh is a badminton player, writer, movie buff, and documentary producer in addition to being a vocalist. He also enjoys cricket, and his favourite athletes are Jonty Rhodes, Lance Klusener, Sourav Ganguly, and Sachin Tendulkar. He appreciates Saina Nehwal in badminton. He is a die-hard supporter of Manchester United and football in general. Additionally, he has stated a desire to perform the World Cup anthem in Qatar in 2022. Arijit Singh has received six Filmfare Awards and a National Award. He has a large following across South Asia, primarily in India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Nepal, and is frequently considered as one of the top vocalists in the Indian music industry. He also holds the title of King of Playback Singing.
